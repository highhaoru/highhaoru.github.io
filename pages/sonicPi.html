Project Description

Project Overview:

"Global Fusion Music Series" is a unique project that merges the timeless melodies of Chinese and Indian music with contemporary electronic rhythms. It includes two distinctive compositions, each representing the musical essence of its respective culture, transformed through electronic synthesis and digital sound design.



Core Technical Aspects:
  • Cultural Music Integration

    • Combines traditional Chinese(Plum Blossom Dreams) and Indian(Lotus Reverie) music elements with electronic music production techniques.


  • Sonic Pi Scripting

    • Utilizes Sonic Pi for live coding and music creation, offering a hands-on approach to music generation.


  • Audio Sampling

    • Incorporates diverse audio samples to enhance the authenticity and richness of the musical experience.
