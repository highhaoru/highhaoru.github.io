Outstanding Performer Award









Outstanding Performer Award Details:
  • Achievement: Outstanding Performer Award
  • Issuing Body: Shanghai Musicians Association Wind Music Committee
  • Date: Summer 2014
  • Recognized For: Exceptional performance in wind music


In the summer of 2014, I was recognized as an Outstanding Performer at the Shanghai Student Wind Orchestra Summer Camp. This honor, bestowed by the Shanghai Musicians Association Wind Music Committee, acknowledged my passion and dedication to wind music.
