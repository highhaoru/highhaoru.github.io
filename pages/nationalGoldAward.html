National Junior Sax Solo Gold Award









Gold Award Details:
  • Achievement: National Junior Sax Solo Gold Award
  • Event: The 9th Dandelion Young Artistic Stars
  • Date: 2009
  • Category: Junior Saxophone Solo


In 2009, I was honored with the prestigious Gold Award in the Junior Saxophone Solo Category at the Dandelion Young Artistic Stars competition, recognizing exceptional young talents in music. The award was a testament to my commitment to the saxophone and underscored my pursuit of musical excellence. It encouraged me to further refine my skills and deepen my artistic expression.
